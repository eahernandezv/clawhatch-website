Questions?

How is this different from ChatGPT? ▾
ChatGPT is an app you visit. ClawHatch is an assistant that lives with you — it remembers everything, messages you proactively, takes real actions like replying to email, and runs on your own private server, all from Telegram.
Do I need an API key or technical knowledge? ▾
No. We handle everything — AI models, server, security, backups, updates. You just chat.
Is my data private? ▾
Your AI runs on a dedicated server only you can access. Your data is never shared, never used for training, and never visible to us unless you grant support access.
Are there usage limits? ▾
Fair-use policy. For normal personal use there are no hard limits. If you need high-volume automation, we'll work with you on a plan.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. No questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What if I don't like it? ▾
Your assistant is live in 5 minutes — you'll know quickly if it's for you. Not happy? Cancel anytime, no commitment. We're confident you'll love it.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▾
We'll email you a full backup of your assistant's memory and data before anything is deleted. Nothing is lost unless you want it to be.
