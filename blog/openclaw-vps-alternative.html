The hidden cost of DIY VPS hosting

The sticker price of a server is not the real price. The real price is your time: setup, patching, debugging, recovering from drift, and explaining to yourself why a thing that worked yesterday is weird today.

What to compare instead of raw monthly cost

  • Time to first useful message
  • Reliability after updates
  • How painful recovery is when something breaks
  • Whether your assistant is actually available from your phone whenever you need it

Who should still self-host

If you love tinkering and want full control, self-hosting is still valid. But if you mainly want a dependable personal AI assistant in Telegram, hosted is usually the less stupid choice.
