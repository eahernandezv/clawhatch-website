Data Deletion

If you want your ClawHatch account data deleted, email ClawHatch.team@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and include the Telegram handle tied to your setup.

What to include

  • Your account email address
  • Your Telegram username or bot setup context
  • A short note that you want your account data deleted

What happens next

We will confirm the request, delete the account data associated with your hosted assistant, and reply when the deletion is complete.
