Seldra ("we", "our", "us") provides personal AI assistants. We are based in the Netherlands and operate under EU law (GDPR). Legal form: Eenmanszaak (sole proprietorship), registration expected March 2026.

DPO: Not required for our size and risk profile. You can still contact us at the address above with any privacy questions.

2) What this policy covers

This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, where we store it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and your rights under GDPR.

3) What we collect

Account and billing

  • Name, email, country (for tax), plan type, subscription status
  • Billing details handled by Stripe (we don't store full card numbers)

Conversations and AI memory

  • Messages you send to the assistant and the assistant's replies
  • Long‑term memory items created to personalize your experience
  • Action logs (e.g., "sent email to …", "booked …", "visited …")

Connected services and devices

  • Metadata for integrations you connect (email/calendar providers, smart devices)
  • Access tokens or keys you provide (stored securely on your dedicated server)

Technical data

  • Server metrics and diagnostics to keep your assistant running
  • Basic website analytics and minimal cookies (see Cookies section)

4) Where your data is stored

Each customer gets a dedicated VPS located in Helsinki, Finland (EU) with Hetzner. Your assistant and its long‑term memory live on that server. We design the system so that we, Seldra, do not need to access your data to operate the service.

Note: If you connect third‑party services (like email providers, calendars, or messaging apps), those services may store or process your data in other regions under their own policies.

5) Who can access your data

  • You (and anyone you invite) can access your assistant and data.
  • Seldra staff do not access your data by default. We can only see your data if you explicitly grant temporary support access to diagnose an issue. You can revoke access at any time.
  • Providers that process data on our behalf (see next section) receive only what's necessary to provide their service.

6) Third‑party processing

To generate AI responses, your prompts, messages, and relevant context are sent to model providers, such as DeepSeek (e.g., DeepSeek V3.2) and Anthropic (e.g., Claude Sonnet 4.6). We may access these models directly or via aggregators like OpenRouter. These providers act as processors when handling your data to deliver the service.

Messaging platforms (Telegram; WhatsApp when available) also process your messages under their own terms and privacy policies. Payments are processed by Stripe. If you connect other integrations (email, calendar, storage, devices), those providers will process data you choose to share with them.

7) Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

  • Contract: We process data to provide the service you subscribe to, including running your assistant and storing its memory.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving reliability, preventing abuse, securing systems, and supporting you when you ask for help.
  • Consent: Where required (e.g., certain cookies, optional marketing). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: Keeping records we must retain for tax or compliance.

8) How long we keep data

  • Active accounts: Conversation history and AI memory are kept indefinitely to provide long‑term personalization, until you delete them or close your account.
  • After cancellation: We retain data for 30 days for export/recovery, then delete it from active systems. Backups cycle out after a limited period.
  • Logs: Technical logs are kept only as long as needed for security and reliability.

9) Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Portability: Request an export in a commonly used format.
  • Objection and restriction: Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten", Article 17).
  • Complaint: You can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@seldra.app. We will respond within the timelines required by law.

10) Data export and deletion

You can request a full export of your conversations, AI memory, and relevant logs. You can also request deletion at any time. If you close your account, we delete data from active systems after 30 days. Some backups may persist for a limited period before being purged; they are kept only for disaster recovery.

11) Security

  • Per‑customer isolation on a dedicated EU server
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive credentials on your server
  • Access controls and audit logs for support access (when you grant it)

No system is perfectly secure. We work to protect your data, and we ask you to use strong authentication and review connected integrations regularly.

12) Cookies and website data

We use minimal cookies on our website to deliver basic functionality (e.g., session, fraud prevention) and optional analytics if you consent. The assistant itself runs primarily in messaging apps and on your server, not in your browser.

13) Children

The service is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

14) International transfers

We store your assistant's data in the EU. If a third‑party provider processes data outside the EU, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) or equivalent mechanisms provided by that provider.

15) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will give reasonable notice (at least 30 days for material changes). Continued use after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
