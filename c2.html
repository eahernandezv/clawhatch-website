Questions?

How is this different from ChatGPT? ▾
ChatGPT is an app you visit. ClawHatch is an assistant that lives with you — it remembers everything, messages you proactively, takes real actions like replying to email, and runs on your own private server, all from Telegram.
What's the difference between Tinker and Essential? ▾
Tinker gives you the full infrastructure — managed server, channels, skills — but you bring your own AI model key (or buy credits from us). Essential includes the AI model, web search, places, voice transcription, and a Welcome Pack of premium credits. If you don't want to deal with API keys, go Essential.
Do I need technical knowledge? ▾
Not for Essential or Builder — we handle everything. Tinker is for people comfortable with API keys. Even then, your AI assistant guides you through every integration.
Is my data private? ▾
Your AI runs on a dedicated server only you can access. Your data is never shared, never used for training, and never visible to us unless you grant support access.
What's the Welcome Pack? ▾
Free one-time credits to try premium features: voice replies, image generation, voice calls, captcha solving, and proxy browsing. Essential gets a starter pack, Builder gets double. After they run out, you can buy more as add-ons.
What are add-ons? ▾
Extra capabilities you can add to any plan: more voice replies, image generation, voice calls, proxy browsing, captcha solving. The Power Pack (€5/mo) bundles heavy proxy and captcha usage. You only pay for what you actually need.
Can I switch plans? ▾
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Your data, memory, and integrations carry over.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. No questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period. We'll email you a backup of your assistant's memory before anything is deleted.
