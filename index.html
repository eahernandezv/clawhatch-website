Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ClawHatch

What is ClawHatch? +
ClawHatch is a managed AI assistant hosting service. We set up and maintain a personal AI assistant for you that runs 24/7 and is accessible through Telegram. Think of it as having your own AI companion with a custom personality, hosted on dedicated infrastructure.
How does it work? +
Simply chat with our onboarding bot (@ClawHatchBot) on Telegram, tell us about your ideal assistant, and we handle all the technical setup. You'll get your own personalized AI that lives on our secure servers and is available to chat with you anytime through Telegram.
Is my data private? +
Absolutely. Your conversations and data are stored on dedicated, secure infrastructure. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your personal information. Your AI assistant is yours alone – no data mixing with other users.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. We believe in flexible, commitment-free service. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
