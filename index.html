Questions?

How is this different from just using the ChatGPT app? ▾
ChatGPT is a passive chatbot you have to visit. ClawHatch is an active agent powered by OpenClaw that lives in your Telegram. It remembers your context across sessions, messages you proactively, executes real workflows (like setting calendar events or reading your code repo), and runs on your own isolated infrastructure.
Do I need to be a developer to set this up? ▾
No. If you know how to copy and paste an API key from OpenAI or Anthropic, you have all the technical skills required. We handle the server provisioning, the Node.js installation, the Telegram Bot API bridge, the skills configuration, and the 24/7 uptime. You just chat.
How does "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) pricing work? ▾
You pay us €19/mo for the hosted infrastructure, daily backups, and the Telegram bridge. You plug in your own API key and pay your provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) directly for tokens. We take zero markup on your usage. You get wholesale AI token pricing without the server hosting headaches.
What happens during the 7-day free trial? ▾
You get 7 days to test the infrastructure with a 15 message/day limit. No credit card is required to start. Plug in your key, test the speed, and experience the Telegram integration. Once you realize how much time this saves you, upgrade to the €19/mo plan to unlock unlimited messaging.
Is my data private? Can you read my prompts? ▾
Your agent runs on a dedicated, isolated server environment. We don't share infrastructure, we don't train models on your data, and we don't read your chats. Your memory files (MEMORY.md) are encrypted and entirely yours to export or delete anytime.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. No friction, no hidden cancellation buttons. If you decide you want to go back to spending your weekends debugging Hetzner servers and API bridges, you can cancel instantly. We'll even email you a backup of your agent's memory files before we spin down your server.
