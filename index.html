Questions?

How is this different from ChatGPT? ▾
ChatGPT is an app you visit. ClawHatch is an assistant that lives with you — it remembers everything, messages you proactively, takes real actions like replying to email, and runs on your own private server, all from Telegram.
Do I need my own API key? ▾
You can bring your own key from any provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) for unlimited use — or buy LLM credits directly from us at prices cheaper than OpenRouter. Either way, we walk you through it in under 60 seconds.
Do I need technical knowledge? ▾
Nope. We handle the server, setup, and updates. You just bring an API key from any AI provider — your assistant walks you through getting one in under 60 seconds.
Is my data private? ▾
Your AI runs on a dedicated server only you can access. Your data is never shared, never used for training, and never visible to us unless you grant support access.
What's included in the €19/mo plan? ▾
Your own private AI assistant on a dedicated server, accessible via Telegram 24/7. Includes 17 built-in skills (weather, PDF, coding, GIFs, RSS, smart home, and more), all self-service integrations (Slack, Notion, Trello, Google Calendar, email...), persistent memory, proactive messaging, and automatic updates. You bring your own LLM API key — or buy credits from us.
What are add-ons? ▾
Optional packs that unlock extra capabilities. Creative Pack (€3/mo): voice-to-text (30/mo), voice replies (20/mo), and image generation (10/mo). Browser-Pro Pack (€5/mo): web search (200/mo), browser automation, proxies, and captcha resolvers (100/mo). LLM Credits: top up from €5, access 30+ models, always cheaper than OpenRouter, no expiry. Each pack includes a monthly cap — your AI will prompt you if you want to top up.
Can I add or remove packs? ▾
Yes, add or remove any pack anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Your data, memory, and integrations always carry over.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. No questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period. We'll email you a backup of your assistant's memory before anything is deleted.
